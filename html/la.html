Every Photo Has A Story To Tell...




Santa Monica Pier

As the stopping point of Route 66, Santa Monica was voted one of the top 10 cities in the world by National Geographic.

The Getty

For the art enthusiests, archetecture diehards, and just the average tourists with an appreciation for beauty, The Getty is an absolute MUST-SEE! There is so much to explore from the always changing art/artifact museums to the Central Garden filled with over 500 different varieties of plants!

Southern California Sunsets

One thing you will notice if you ever happen to be on the beach during the sunset, is that everyone is watching too. Once the sun disappears under the pacific, everyone packs up and heads home...or maybe to dinner! But in all truth, a sunset is Los Angeles are some of the most beautiful sunsets you will ever witness.

The Broad

Another one for my art gallery-goers. If its your first time to LA, The Broad must be on your bucket list! Little tip: make sure to see the Infinity Mirrored room and sign up first thing so you dont have to wait FOREVERR!

Where To Eat...

North Italia

Looking for a delicious taste of italy right off the golden coast? This is 100% the spot to go! Personally, I recommend for apetizer the White Truffle Garlic Bread and for entree the Spicy Rigatoni Vodka pasta! *mwah* chefs kiss

Shake Shack Crystal Cove

When your heading south on Pacific Coast Highway, keep your eyes peeled for this deliciously hidden gem right off PCH, literally. Shake and burger are always a good combo!

Tequilla Jacks Long Beach

The best - and I mean THE BEST - black beans I have ever tasted! I know I know, "black beans dont really have a taste" well sweetheart, here they do and they taste BOMB! Oh yeah, their other food is pretty good too, yummy chips and salsa!

Nelson's at Terranea

If you are looking for a breezy brunch with a little adventure, this is your place. Terranea is welcome to not just guests but anyone wanting to enjoy a taste of luxury with a delicious lunch. Make sure to adventure out and walk down their trails to find some hidden gems!
