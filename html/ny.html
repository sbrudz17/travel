The Metro

One of the dirtiest, smelliest, aesthetic, new york vibey places you will ever experience. Anyone that visits New York City has to ride the subway and get lost a couple of times to truly get the whole NYC tourist experience. Don't stress about it, ask a local (if they actual know what they are talking about) and don't give directions to other tourist acting like you know what you are talking about and accidentally send a stranger on the wrong train....yeah I was one of those people...Sorry guy in the red hoodie in NYC October 2019!!!
